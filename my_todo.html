Personal Adventure TODO List

"Life is either a great adventure or nothing." - Helen Keller

I don't want to turn 80 and look back on life wondering "why didn't I try that?" So, I am keeping a list of personal adventures I would like to do.

  1. Hike (part of) the Appalachian Trail

    The Appalachian Trail is the longest hiking-only footpath in the world, ranging from Maine to Georgia. Every year hundreds of people set out on the 3-Month trek spanning the East coast of the United States. I do not have that much time to dedicate, all at once. But, I would like to spend a few days/weeks per year hiking portions of the trail.

  2. Learn to Fly a Helicopter

    Flying in an airplane is actually one of my least favorite things to do... ever. But, I am really interested in taking an aerial tour of NYC and other cities. The best way to do this is by Helicopter. Plus, if I learn to fly a chopper I will be a crucial asset during the Zombie apocalypse.

  3. Live in a European Country

    This one kind of explains itself. I have spent a lot of time visiting Europe, but I've always felt like a tourist. I would love to spend at least three years living and working in Europe.
